Project Timeline

Milestones & Progress.

From project initiation to final deployment — every key milestone on the journey.

June 2024

Project Proposal Approved

Research topic finalised and supervisor approval obtained. Initial literature review commenced covering sports analytics, pose estimation, and action recognition.

Completed
August 2024

Dataset Collection & Annotation

Captured practice-session footage from cricket nets. Annotated shot types, delivery lengths, and action legality labels. Built the initial training dataset.

Completed
October 2024

Pose Estimation Pipeline

Integrated MediaPipe Pose for real-time body landmark extraction. Built the frame extraction and calibration pipeline using stump detection.

Completed
December 2024

Shot Classification Model v1

Trained temporal convolutional network on pose-landmark sequences. Achieved initial classification accuracy across six shot categories.

Completed
February 2025

Bowling Analysis Module

Implemented ball tracking with YOLOv8 and homography-based pitch mapping. Delivered speed estimation, length classification, and trajectory visualisation.

Completed
March 2025

Mobile App Development

Built cross-platform React Native app with session management, video upload, calibration UI, and result dashboards for both batting and bowling analysis.

Completed
May 2025

Final Evaluation & Deployment

End-to-end system testing, user evaluation with cricket coaches, performance benchmarking, and final thesis submission.

Upcoming

AI-Powered Cricket Analysis — a research initiative on intelligent video-based performance evaluation.
